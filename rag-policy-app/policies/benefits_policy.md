# Employee Benefits Policy

**Document ID:** HR-007  
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2024  
**Department:** Human Resources

---

## 1. Overview

TechCorp Inc. offers a comprehensive benefits package to all eligible full-time employees. Benefits are effective on the **first day of the month following 30 days of employment**.

## 2. Health Insurance

### 2.1 Medical Insurance
TechCorp offers three tiers of medical coverage through BlueCross BlueShield:

| Plan | Monthly Premium (Employee) | Monthly Premium (Employee + Family) | Deductible |
|---|---|---|---|
| Basic PPO | $0 (company pays 100%) | $150 | $2,500 |
| Standard PPO | $0 (company pays 100%) | $100 | $1,500 |
| Premium HMO | $0 (company pays 100%) | $50 | $500 |

- Employees must enroll within **30 days** of hire date.
- Open enrollment is held each November for coverage starting January 1.

### 2.2 Dental Insurance
- TechCorp covers **100% of premiums** for employee-only dental coverage.
- Family dental coverage: $30/month additional.
- Annual maximum benefit: $2,000 per person.
- Orthodontia covered at 50% up to a lifetime maximum of $1,500.

### 2.3 Vision Insurance
- TechCorp covers **100% of premiums** for employee-only vision coverage.
- Family vision coverage: $10/month additional.
- Annual eye exam: fully covered.
- Frames/contacts allowance: $200/year.

## 3. Retirement Benefits

### 3.1 401(k) Plan
- Employees are eligible to contribute to the company 401(k) plan from **day 1 of employment**.
- TechCorp matches **100% of employee contributions up to 4% of salary**.
- Employer match vests over a **3-year graded schedule**:
  - Year 1: 33% vested
  - Year 2: 67% vested
  - Year 3: 100% vested
- The 2024 IRS 401(k) contribution limit is $23,000 ($30,500 if age 50+).

## 4. Life and Disability Insurance

- **Basic Life Insurance:** TechCorp provides 1x annual salary in life insurance at no cost to the employee.
- **Supplemental Life Insurance:** Employees may purchase additional coverage up to 5x salary at group rates.
- **Short-Term Disability (STD):** Covers 60% of salary for up to **12 weeks** following a qualifying disability, after a 7-day waiting period.
- **Long-Term Disability (LTD):** Covers 60% of salary after STD expires, up to age 65 or recovery.

## 5. Employee Wellness Program

- **Wellness Stipend:** $50/month reimbursement for gym memberships, fitness apps, or wellness classes.
- **Mental Health:** TechCorp provides access to **8 free counseling sessions per year** through Lyra Health (our Employee Assistance Program provider).
- **Employee Assistance Program (EAP):** Free confidential counseling, financial advice, and legal consultations through Lyra Health. Available 24/7 at 1-800-555-5972.

## 6. Parental Leave

- **Primary Caregiver Leave:** 16 weeks of fully paid leave for the primary caregiver following birth, adoption, or foster placement.
- **Secondary Caregiver Leave:** 4 weeks of fully paid leave for the secondary caregiver.
- Parental leave must be taken within **12 months** of the qualifying event.
- Employees must notify HR at least **30 days** before the expected leave start date.

## 7. Professional Development

- All employees (after 90-day probation) receive **$500/year** for professional development (courses, certifications, books, conferences).
- An additional **$1,000/year** is available for job-related degree coursework with manager + HR approval.
- Submit development expenses via the expense portal (FIN-001).

## 8. Other Benefits

- **Commuter Benefits:** Pre-tax commuter benefit up to $315/month for transit and parking.
- **Employee Referral Program:** $1,500 bonus paid after a referred employee completes 90 days.
- **Volunteer Time Off:** 2 paid days per year for volunteering at approved non-profit organizations.
- **Employee Discount:** 30% discount on TechCorp products for personal use.

## 9. Benefits During Leave

- Benefits continue during approved paid leaves of absence.
- During unpaid leaves exceeding 30 days, employees are responsible for paying their portion of premiums (COBRA options available).
