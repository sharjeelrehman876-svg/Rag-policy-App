# Data Privacy Policy

**Document ID:** IT-002  
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2024  
**Department:** Information Technology / Legal

---

## 1. Purpose

TechCorp Inc. is committed to protecting the privacy of its employees, customers, and partners. This policy outlines how TechCorp collects, uses, stores, and protects personal data in compliance with applicable laws including GDPR, CCPA, and other regional data protection regulations.

## 2. Scope

This policy applies to all employees, contractors, and third-party vendors who handle personal data on behalf of TechCorp Inc.

## 3. Types of Personal Data We Process

### 3.1 Employee Data
- Contact information (name, address, phone, email)
- Financial data (salary, bank account for payroll)
- Performance and disciplinary records
- Health information (for benefits and leave purposes only)
- Government ID and tax information

### 3.2 Customer Data
- Contact and billing information
- Usage and behavioral data
- Support interaction records

## 4. Principles of Data Processing

TechCorp processes personal data in accordance with the following principles:

1. **Lawfulness, Fairness, and Transparency:** Data is processed with a lawful basis (consent, contract, legal obligation, or legitimate interest).
2. **Purpose Limitation:** Data collected for a specific purpose is not used for unrelated purposes.
3. **Data Minimization:** Only data necessary for the stated purpose is collected.
4. **Accuracy:** Personal data is kept accurate and up to date.
5. **Storage Limitation:** Data is retained only as long as necessary (see Section 6).
6. **Integrity and Confidentiality:** Data is protected with appropriate technical and organizational security measures (see IT-001).

## 5. Employee Rights Regarding Personal Data

Employees have the following rights under GDPR and CCPA:
- **Right to Access:** Request a copy of personal data TechCorp holds about them.
- **Right to Rectification:** Request correction of inaccurate personal data.
- **Right to Erasure:** Request deletion of personal data (subject to legal obligations).
- **Right to Data Portability:** Receive personal data in a structured, machine-readable format.
- **Right to Object:** Object to processing based on legitimate interests.

To exercise these rights, submit a request to privacy@techcorp.com. Requests will be fulfilled within **30 days**.

## 6. Data Retention Schedule

| Data Type | Retention Period |
|---|---|
| Employee records (active) | Duration of employment + 7 years |
| Payroll records | 7 years after last payment |
| Performance reviews | Duration of employment + 3 years |
| CCTV footage | 30 days |
| Customer data | Duration of contract + 5 years |
| Marketing data | 2 years from last interaction |
| Incident reports | 5 years |

## 7. Data Sharing

- Employee data is **never sold** to third parties.
- Data may be shared with service providers (payroll processors, benefits administrators) under strict Data Processing Agreements.
- Data may be disclosed to law enforcement or regulators if required by law.
- International data transfers comply with applicable transfer mechanisms (e.g., Standard Contractual Clauses for GDPR).

## 8. Data Breach Response

In the event of a data breach:
- The IT Security team must be notified within **1 hour** of discovery.
- Affected individuals and regulators (where required by law) will be notified within **72 hours** of TechCorp becoming aware of the breach.
- A breach response plan will be activated immediately.

## 9. Third-Party Vendors

All third-party vendors with access to TechCorp personal data must:
- Sign a Data Processing Agreement (DPA) before receiving any data.
- Demonstrate compliance with applicable data protection laws.
- Undergo a security review by the IT team annually.
