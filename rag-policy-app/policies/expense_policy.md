# Expense Reimbursement Policy

**Document ID:** FIN-001  
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2024  
**Department:** Finance

---

## 1. Purpose

This policy governs the reimbursement of business expenses incurred by TechCorp Inc. employees. All expenses must be business-related, reasonable, and properly documented.

## 2. General Principles

- Expenses must be **pre-approved** by a manager for amounts exceeding $500.
- All expense claims must be submitted within **30 days** of the expense being incurred.
- Receipts are required for all expenses over **$25**.
- Personal expenses will not be reimbursed under any circumstances.

## 3. Travel Expenses

### 3.1 Airfare
- Economy class is required for flights under **6 hours**.
- Business class may be approved for flights over 6 hours with VP-level approval.
- Flights must be booked at least **14 days in advance** unless travel is urgent.
- Book through the company's preferred travel portal (travel.techcorp-internal.com).

### 3.2 Hotel Accommodation
- Hotel stays are reimbursed up to **$200 per night** in standard cities.
- In high-cost cities (New York, San Francisco, London, Tokyo), the limit is **$300 per night**.
- Use company preferred hotels where available for negotiated rates.

### 3.3 Ground Transportation
- Taxis, rideshares (Uber/Lyft): fully reimbursable for business travel.
- Personal vehicle mileage: reimbursed at the **IRS standard mileage rate** (updated annually).
- Rental cars: economy class only; insurance through the corporate card is included.

### 3.4 Meals During Travel
- Breakfast: up to **$20**
- Lunch: up to **$25**
- Dinner: up to **$50**
- Alcohol is not reimbursable unless it is part of a client entertainment event with manager approval.

## 4. Client Entertainment

- Client meals and entertainment are reimbursable up to **$150 per person**.
- The expense report must include: names of all attendees, their company affiliations, and the business purpose.
- VP approval is required for client entertainment events exceeding **$1,000 total**.

## 5. Technology and Equipment

- Employees may expense up to **$100/year** on productivity software or apps without prior approval.
- Equipment purchases (monitors, keyboards, etc.) over $50 require manager pre-approval.
- All company-purchased equipment remains the property of TechCorp Inc.

## 6. Conference and Training Fees

- Conference registration fees are reimbursable with manager approval.
- Online courses and certifications: up to **$500/year** per employee without additional approval.
- Costs beyond $500 require manager + HR approval.

## 7. Expense Submission Process

1. Collect all receipts (digital or physical).
2. Submit claims through the **Expense Portal** (expenses.techcorp-internal.com).
3. Attach receipts to each line item.
4. Manager reviews and approves within 5 business days.
5. Finance processes approved expenses within **10 business days**.
6. Reimbursement is made via direct deposit to the employee's payroll account.

## 8. Non-Reimbursable Expenses

The following are never reimbursable:
- Personal entertainment (movies, personal trips, etc.)
- Fines and penalties (parking tickets, traffic violations)
- Gym memberships (unless part of an approved wellness program)
- Gifts for personal occasions
- Expenses without receipts over $25

## 9. Policy Violations

Submitting false or inflated expense claims is considered fraud and may result in disciplinary action up to and including termination, and may be referred to law enforcement.

## 10. Contact

For expense policy questions, contact finance@techcorp.com or extension 1200.
