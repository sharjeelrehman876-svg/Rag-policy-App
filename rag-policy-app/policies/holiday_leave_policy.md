# Holiday and Leave Policy

**Document ID:** HR-005  
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2024  
**Department:** Human Resources

---

## 1. Company-Observed Holidays

TechCorp Inc. observes the following **11 paid holidays** per year. Employees are not required to work on these days and will receive full pay.

| Holiday | Date |
|---|---|
| New Year's Day | January 1 |
| Martin Luther King Jr. Day | Third Monday of January |
| Presidents' Day | Third Monday of February |
| Memorial Day | Last Monday of May |
| Juneteenth | June 19 |
| Independence Day | July 4 |
| Labor Day | First Monday of September |
| Thanksgiving Day | Fourth Thursday of November |
| Day After Thanksgiving | Fourth Friday of November |
| Christmas Eve | December 24 |
| Christmas Day | December 25 |

### 1.1 Holiday Observance Rules
- If a holiday falls on a **Saturday**, it is observed on the preceding **Friday**.
- If a holiday falls on a **Sunday**, it is observed on the following **Monday**.
- Remote and in-office employees both receive the same holiday benefits.

## 2. Floating Holidays

- Each employee receives **2 floating holidays** per calendar year.
- Floating holidays can be used for religious observances, cultural celebrations, or any personal reason.
- Floating holidays do not carry over to the next year and are forfeited if unused by December 31.
- Floating holidays must be requested via the HR portal with at least **3 business days' notice**.

## 3. Bereavement Leave

Employees are entitled to paid bereavement leave upon the death of a family member:

| Relationship | Paid Leave |
|---|---|
| Spouse / Domestic Partner | 5 days |
| Child (including step/adopted) | 5 days |
| Parent (including step) | 5 days |
| Sibling | 3 days |
| Grandparent | 3 days |
| In-law (parent/sibling) | 2 days |
| Other close relative | 1 day |

- Bereavement leave does not count against the employee's PTO balance.
- Additional unpaid bereavement leave may be granted at the manager's discretion.
- Employees may be required to provide documentation (e.g., obituary or death certificate) upon return.

## 4. Jury Duty Leave

- Employees summoned for jury duty will receive **full pay** for up to **10 business days**.
- Beyond 10 days, employees may use PTO or take unpaid leave for the duration of jury service.
- Employees must notify their manager and HR as soon as they receive a jury summons.
- A copy of the jury summons must be submitted to HR.

## 5. Voting Leave

- Employees are entitled to **2 hours of paid leave** to vote in federal, state, or local elections.
- Voting leave should be scheduled at the beginning or end of the workday to minimize disruption.
- Employees must notify their manager at least **2 days** before the election day.

## 6. Military Leave

- TechCorp complies fully with the Uniformed Services Employment and Reemployment Rights Act (USERRA).
- Employees called to active military duty will receive differential pay (the difference between military pay and their TechCorp salary) for up to **30 days** per year.
- Military leave does not affect accrued PTO or benefits.

## 7. Unpaid Leave of Absence

- Employees who have exhausted all PTO and require additional time off may apply for an unpaid leave of absence.
- Unpaid leave of up to **4 weeks** may be approved by a department head.
- Longer unpaid leaves require HR and VP approval.
- Benefits continue during unpaid leaves of up to 30 days. Beyond 30 days, employees may need to arrange COBRA coverage.

## 8. Contact

For all leave-related inquiries, contact hr@techcorp.com or extension 1100.
