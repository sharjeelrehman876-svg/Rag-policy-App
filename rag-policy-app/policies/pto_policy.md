# Paid Time Off (PTO) Policy

**Document ID:** HR-001  
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2024  
**Department:** Human Resources

---

## 1. Overview

TechCorp Inc. provides all full-time employees with a generous Paid Time Off (PTO) program. This policy outlines the accrual, usage, and management of PTO for all eligible employees.

## 2. Eligibility

- All full-time employees working 30+ hours per week are eligible for PTO.
- Part-time employees working 20–29 hours per week receive 50% of the standard PTO allowance.
- Contractors and temporary staff are not eligible for PTO benefits.

## 3. PTO Accrual Rates

| Years of Service | PTO Days Per Year |
|---|---|
| 0–1 year | 15 days |
| 1–3 years | 18 days |
| 3–5 years | 22 days |
| 5+ years | 25 days |

PTO accrues at the beginning of each calendar month. New employees begin accruing PTO from their first day of employment but may not use PTO during the first 90-day probationary period.

## 4. PTO Request Process

1. Employees must submit PTO requests via the HR portal at least **5 business days** in advance for requests of 3 or more days.
2. For single-day PTO, employees must notify their direct manager **24 hours** in advance when possible.
3. Emergency PTO may be taken without prior notice; the employee must notify their manager as soon as possible, and within 2 hours of the start of the workday.
4. All PTO requests are subject to manager approval based on business needs.

## 5. PTO Carryover

- Employees may carry over a maximum of **10 PTO days** from one calendar year to the next.
- Unused PTO beyond 10 days will be forfeited at year-end (December 31).
- Carried-over PTO must be used by **March 31** of the following year or it will expire.

## 6. PTO Payout Upon Separation

- Employees who resign or are terminated will receive a payout for all **unused accrued PTO** up to the carryover maximum of 10 days.
- PTO payout will be included in the final paycheck.
- Employees who are terminated for cause may forfeit their PTO payout at the discretion of senior management.

## 7. Sick Leave vs. PTO

- TechCorp does not maintain a separate sick leave bank. Sick days are deducted from the PTO balance.
- If an employee exhausts all PTO due to a medical condition, they may apply for unpaid medical leave under the Family and Medical Leave Act (FMLA).

## 8. Holidays

- Company-observed holidays (see HR-005) do not count against an employee's PTO balance.
- If a holiday falls on a weekend, it will be observed on the nearest weekday.
