# Remote Work Policy

**Document ID:** HR-002  
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2024  
**Department:** Human Resources

---

## 1. Purpose

This policy establishes guidelines for remote work arrangements at TechCorp Inc. It ensures productivity, collaboration, and security are maintained while providing employees flexibility.

## 2. Eligibility for Remote Work

- Employees must have completed their **90-day probationary period** before being eligible for remote work.
- Employees must be in **good standing** (no active Performance Improvement Plans).
- The employee's role must be deemed suitable for remote work by their department head.
- Employees on a formal corrective action plan are not eligible for remote work.

## 3. Remote Work Arrangements

### 3.1 Hybrid Model (Standard)
- Employees are expected to work **in-office a minimum of 3 days per week** (Tuesday, Wednesday, and Thursday are preferred in-office days).
- The remaining 2 days may be worked remotely.

### 3.2 Fully Remote (Exception-Based)
- Fully remote arrangements are available only for roles explicitly designated as remote-eligible.
- Fully remote employees must be available to travel to the office **at least once per quarter** for team meetings, at the company's expense.

## 4. Core Hours

- All employees (remote or in-office) must be available and responsive during **core hours: 10:00 AM – 3:00 PM** in their local time zone.
- Meetings may be scheduled within core hours without prior employee approval.
- Outside of core hours, employees have flexibility in scheduling their work.

## 5. Equipment and Home Office

- TechCorp will provide a **standard equipment package** to remote employees: laptop, headset, and external monitor.
- Employees are responsible for providing a reliable internet connection of at least **25 Mbps download / 10 Mbps upload**.
- The company will reimburse up to **$50/month** for home internet costs for fully remote employees. Submit via the expense policy (FIN-001).
- Employees are responsible for the security of company equipment in their home.

## 6. Security Requirements for Remote Work

- All remote work must be done on a **company-provided or approved device**.
- Employees must use the company **VPN** when accessing internal systems remotely.
- Public Wi-Fi networks (e.g., cafes, airports) are prohibited for work unless the VPN is active.
- Employees must ensure their home workspace is private and that confidential information cannot be overheard or viewed by others.

## 7. Performance and Accountability

- Remote employees are held to the same performance standards as in-office employees.
- Managers will conduct **monthly check-ins** specifically for remote employees to discuss workload and address any concerns.
- Persistent failure to be available during core hours or to meet performance standards may result in revocation of remote work privileges.

## 8. Revocation of Remote Work Privileges

The company reserves the right to revoke remote work arrangements at any time with **2 weeks' notice** if business needs require it, or immediately if there is a violation of this policy.

## 9. Contact

For remote work requests, submit a formal request via the HR portal. Questions may be directed to hr@techcorp.com or your direct manager.
