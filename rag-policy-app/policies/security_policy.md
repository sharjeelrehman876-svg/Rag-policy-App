# Information Security Policy

**Document ID:** IT-001  
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2024  
**Department:** Information Technology

---

## 1. Purpose

This policy establishes the security standards that all TechCorp Inc. employees, contractors, and third parties must adhere to when accessing or handling company data and systems.

## 2. Scope

This policy applies to:
- All full-time and part-time employees
- Contractors and consultants with system access
- Third-party vendors with access to TechCorp data

## 3. Password Policy

- Passwords must be at least **12 characters** long.
- Passwords must include: uppercase letters, lowercase letters, numbers, and at least one special character.
- Passwords must be changed every **90 days**.
- Employees must not reuse any of the last **10 passwords**.
- Passwords must **never** be shared with colleagues, managers, or IT staff.
- Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) is **mandatory** for all company systems.

## 4. Device Security

- All company devices must have **full-disk encryption** enabled.
- Devices must auto-lock after **5 minutes** of inactivity.
- Only company-approved software may be installed on company devices.
- Lost or stolen devices must be reported to IT Security within **1 hour** of discovery.
- IT has the right to remotely wipe any company device in the event of loss, theft, or termination.

## 5. Data Classification

| Level | Description | Examples |
|---|---|---|
| Public | Approved for public release | Marketing materials, press releases |
| Internal | For employee use only | Policies, internal announcements |
| Confidential | Restricted access, business-sensitive | Financial data, customer records |
| Restricted | Highest sensitivity, strictly controlled | PII, source code, trade secrets |

- Restricted and Confidential data must be encrypted in transit and at rest.
- Confidential or Restricted data must not be stored on personal devices or personal cloud storage (Google Drive, Dropbox personal accounts, etc.).

## 6. Email and Communication Security

- Employees must not click links or open attachments from unknown senders.
- Phishing emails must be reported to security@techcorp.com immediately.
- Sensitive data must not be sent via unencrypted email.
- Use company-approved encrypted messaging for sharing Confidential or Restricted data.

## 7. Internet and Network Use

- Company internet is provided for business purposes. Limited personal use is acceptable.
- The following sites are prohibited: illegal content, gambling, hacking tools, and streaming services for personal entertainment during work hours.
- All remote access must use the company **VPN** (see Remote Work Policy HR-002).
- Public Wi-Fi must only be used with the VPN active.

## 8. Access Control

- Access to systems is granted on a **least-privilege basis** — employees receive only the access required for their role.
- Access reviews are conducted **every 6 months**.
- When an employee leaves the company, all access is revoked within **24 hours** of their last day.
- Employees must not share login credentials or access cards.

## 9. Incident Reporting

Employees must immediately report any of the following:
- Suspected data breaches or unauthorized access
- Malware or ransomware infections
- Loss or theft of company devices
- Suspicious emails or phishing attempts

**Report incidents to:** security@techcorp.com | Emergency Hotline: ext. 9999

## 10. Security Training

- All employees must complete the **annual security awareness training** within 30 days of hire and once every year thereafter.
- Failure to complete mandatory training may result in system access being suspended.

## 11. Consequences of Policy Violations

Violations of this policy may result in disciplinary action up to and including termination. Violations that constitute criminal activity will be reported to the appropriate authorities.
